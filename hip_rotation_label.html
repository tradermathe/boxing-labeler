What to watch

Score the hip turn on the punch, not the whole body. It reduces to two checkpoints:
1) does the rear heel pivot off the floor?
2) do the hips drive through toward the target (belt buckle ends up facing it), or just twitch?
Watch at 0.25–0.5× and loop a few times before committing. Skip ([S]) if you genuinely can't tell.
