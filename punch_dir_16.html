Conventions

Pick the boxer's facing direction. Dial is a top-down compass — camera at the bottom.
0° (bottom) = facing the camera (chest toward you).
±90° (sides) = sideways (one shoulder at camera).
180° (top) = back to camera.
+ = turned to the boxer's right, − = to the left.
[n] keys hit the 8 major bins; click for the 22.5° in-betweens.
skip (center, [2]) = can't tell / occluded.

Shortcuts: 1–9 = 8 major bins (numpad layout)  |  click dial = 22.5° bins  |  Space = Play/Pause  |  ←/→ = ±1 frame  |  N = Next  |  P = Previous  |  F = First  |  U = Next unlabeled  |  C = Clear
